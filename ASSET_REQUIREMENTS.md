# Future Asset Requirements

The current website uses the supplied logo at `public/brand/logo.jpeg` and branded CSS placeholders where official assets are not yet available.

Recommended future assets:

- Transparent SVG logo
- Dark-background logo variation
- Official leadership portraits
- Confirmed partner logos
- Product catalogue covers
- Healthcare-zoning diagram
- Official office/location photography
- Open Graph image

Do not add fictional partner brands, leadership portraits, product packaging, certifications, client logos, testimonials, phone numbers, or street addresses.
